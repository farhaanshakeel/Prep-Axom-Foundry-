// 01 — About

What Is PAF?

The Prep Axiom Foundry is a peer-led global academic community built for students serious about olympiad and other competitive exams, especially mathematics and physics, including biology and chemistry. A community of students.

Here, students form study groups, teach each other, take moderated tests, and compete in weekly challenges — all while being supported by mentors who have walked the same path.

We also believe that academic excellence and mental wellbeing go hand in hand. Our Student Advocate is always available — because no student should burn out preparing alone.

"PAF was founded by two students — who are aiming higher and are great enthusiasts of science and mathematics. Both wanted a community where students grow together despite having personal family pressures or problems. Now there is." — Auvalen, Founder — Nuven, Co-Founder
